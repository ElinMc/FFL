How it works

1

Add your favorite recipes

Type a name, pick suggested ingredients (or let the app guess), pick a category, save!

2

Spin the roulette

Tap SPIN — cards shuffle, the winner is announced with a 6-7 celebration and confetti.

3

Plan your week

Drag meals onto your calendar or auto-fill the whole week with one tap.

4

Make the shopping list

All ingredients combined, deduplicated, and scaled for your family size. Tick off what you have.
